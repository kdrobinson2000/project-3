Favorite Chicago Places!

Chicago is only technically a Midwestern city. It's certainly more interesting than 90% of the Midwestern cities :-).

#1 VISIT: The Art Institute of Chicago

The Art Institute of Chicago is my favorite place in Chicago! As a child, it ignited my love for design. I'm always in awe of the human capacity to produce beauty. The always have exhibits that far exceed my imagination.

#2 EAT: The Park Grill

When I'm already downtown, this is my go-to spot. They have the BEST hamburgers! It's the best way to end a visit downtown:-)

#3 SEE: Cload Gate

After eating at The Grill, walk outside and enjoy Chicago's favorite attraction. Most call it The Bean, but it's good trivia to know it's really called Cloud Gate. The Bean sits in is a very scenic park. It's a peaceful place to walk and people watch:-)
